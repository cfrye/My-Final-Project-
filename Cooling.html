Cooling

Are you ever playing a sport and it's just freezing outside or in the gym? Our product recognizes your body temperature as cold and produce heat so you can stay warm.

Dangers Of Getting Very Cold

It's easy to get cold quickly if you are outside in wet, windy, or cold weather.Low temperatures put a greater load on your metabolism - As temperatures fall, so blood pressure increases as blood is taken away from the skin surface increasing the amount in the core.When it's warm and you sweat, it makes you feel thirsty as a reminder to replace the lost fluid. In the cold, this mechanism doesn't work so well and counter-intuitively it can be make it more likely to become dehydrated when exercising in cold than hot conditions.
